Quesadilla

This quesadilla comes from the website, allrecipes.com and was originally given by Jackie. Cheese Quesadillas are easy to make meals that can be consumed during lunch and usually include cheese covered by the tortilla bases put to make a scrumptious quick and easy meal. For extra taste, spice, or seasoning, consider adding Pico de Gallo as a suitable way of making it even tastier

Ingredients

  • 2 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Place one flour tortilla onto a large microwave-safe plate. Sprinkle with Cheddar, then top with remaining tortilla. Microwave on high until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
  2. Slice like a pizza to serve.
