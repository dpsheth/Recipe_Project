Pasta

This pasta comes from the website, allrecipes.com and was originally given by Lauren. The pasta in particular is called pesto pasta and has a greenish yellow look to it as a result of using pesto

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
